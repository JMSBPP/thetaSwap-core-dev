Revised: Crossbar as X-Axis + Curve Emerging Above

The Θ horizontal bar is always present (straight line). The power-law curve rises from it like a distribution plotted on its axis.

Construction

  • Horizontal crossbar (straight): Always present — this IS the theta bar. Spans full ellipse width. Gives the Θ read at any size.
  • Power-law curve: Emerges upward from the crossbar. Starts at the left edge of the bar, peaks, then decays back down to the bar on the right. The crossbar is its x-axis.
  • Color version: Crossbar in stone (matches arcs), curve in lime — the data rises from the structure.
  • Monochrome: Both crossbar and curve in foreground color, differentiated by opacity or weight.
