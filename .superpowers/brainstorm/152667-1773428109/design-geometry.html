Design Section 1: Core Geometry

The foundational construction of the Split Equilibrium logomark

Construction

  • Base grid: 120×120 unit viewBox, centered at (60, 60)
  • Outer circle: radius 33 units, split vertically at x=60
  • Left half-arc: Full opacity — represents passive LPs (the protected)
  • Right half-arc: 50% opacity — represents sophisticated agents (JITs)
  • Vertical bisector (lime): Runs full height of the circle (y: 24→96) — the protocol as equilibrium mechanism
  • Horizontal crossbar: 30% opacity, spans the full diameter — the "ghost of theta" that identifies the Θ letterform
  • Stroke weight: 2.5 units for primary elements, 1.5 for secondary

Semantic Mapping

  • Left arc (bright): Passive LPs — the ones being protected. Full presence.
  • Right arc (faded): Sophisticated agents (JIT, MEV). Present but diminished — their concentration is being measured and counteracted.
  • Vertical lime line: ThetaSwap itself — the equilibrating protocol that stands between the two forces.
  • Horizontal crossbar (ghost): The Θ identity — subtly confirms "this is theta" without competing with the narrative.
