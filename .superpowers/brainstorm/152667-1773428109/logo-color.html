Color treatment for the logomark?

Geometric minimalism works best with restrained color. How should the logo handle light/dark modes and accent?

A

Pure Monochrome

Stone-900 on light, Stone-50 on dark. No accent color ever. Maximum austerity — lets the geometry speak. Like a mathematical symbol.

B

Monochrome + Lime Accent

Primary mark in stone monochrome, but one element highlighted in lime-500 (the existing accent color). The accent could mark the "protection threshold" or equilibrium point in the design.

C

Two-Tone Stone

Uses two weights of stone (e.g., stone-900 + stone-400) to create depth and hierarchy within the mark. Subtle but adds visual interest without introducing new colors.
