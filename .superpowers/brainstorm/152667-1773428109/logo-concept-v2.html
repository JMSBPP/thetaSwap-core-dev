Refined: What should the logomark communicate?

The name "Theta" references the time-derivative, but the product is insurance against adverse competition. The logo should bridge both.

Context

Theta (Θ) = position turnover rate = how "sophisticated" an agent is. High θ = JIT predator. The protocol measures concentration of fee capture by these agents and insures passive LPs against it.

A

Θ as Shield / Barrier

The theta symbol reimagined as a protective barrier — the horizontal bar of Θ becomes a dividing line between passive LPs (protected) and sophisticated agents (above). Insurance meets math.

B

Concentration Gradient with Threshold

A stylized HHI curve or distribution showing fee concentration — with a clear threshold line (the "protection level"). Communicates: we measure, we protect. Data-driven and economic.

C

Θ Embedded in a Distribution

The theta letterform integrated into or emerging from a bell curve / power-law distribution shape. Bridges the mathematical identity (Θ) with the economic concept (fee concentration distribution).

D

Abstract: Equilibrium / Balance

Two opposing forces in balance — representing the tension between passive LPs and sophisticated agents, with the protocol as the equilibrating mechanism. Minimal, geometric, institutional.
