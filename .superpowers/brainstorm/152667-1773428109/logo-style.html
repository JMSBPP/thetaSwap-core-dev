What visual style should the logomark have?

The logo needs to fit the existing design system: stone neutrals, DM Sans, institutional feel. But within that, there's range.

A

Geometric Minimalism

Pure geometric shapes — circles, lines, precise angles. Stark, clean, high contrast. Think: academic journal cover, institutional finance. Monochrome stone palette only.

B

Refined Line Art

Thin, precise strokes (matching Lucide icon weight ~1.5px). Elegant, technical, like an engineering diagram or mathematical figure. Pairs naturally with IBM Plex Mono.

C

Gradient / Depth

Subtle stone-to-lime gradient or layered opacity. Adds dimensionality while staying within the palette. More modern, slightly warmer. Stands out from pure monochrome DeFi logos.

D

Bold Glyph

A thick, confident, custom-drawn symbol — like a stamp or seal. Heavy presence at small sizes (favicon, app icon). Authoritative and distinctive.
